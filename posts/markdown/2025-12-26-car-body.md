If you don't already know, I am doing a blog series where I try to make a car using Raspberry Pi and Arduino (Arduino to control the motors, Raspberry Pi to make it smart, no I can't use the Pi to control motors).

Before, I didn't know what I was going to do "smart" to the car, but now I have an idea: make it controllable on the web. I can just setup the Pi as a server and port forward the URL (I understand it's never just a "just").
