# Robot Hustle
## Intro
Not much done this week just research.

## What happened
So I have made a vibe coded YT thumbnail A/B tester, but now I think for side hustles that isn't the right play. Why?
1. I don't like web dev in the first place
2. I love robotics dev
3. Robotics dev projects are way more easy to think of and fun

Think about it — when you want to make a good web dev project, you are stumped. But when you want to innovate, make a robot that does something no one has ever made, it's so easy. It's because the most generic and obvious ideas are just not taken in robotics, it's just cuz they're hard to make. I could think of one right now — a robot to take your laundry from the laundry basket to the washing machine. BOOM! I know it takes entire companies to make such things, but if you can pull off a rudimentary design Elon Musk will be on his knees.
