Car

If you don't already know, I am doing a blog series where I try to make a car using Raspberry Pi and Arduino (Arduino to control the motors, Raspberry Pi to make it smart, no I can't use the Pi to control motors).

Insight

Before I explain my changes to my car, I would like to share an epiphany I had in the making of the car (it's about where I want to take this project). Before, I didn't know what I was going to do "smart" to the car, but now I have an idea: make it controllable on the web. I can just setup the Pi as a server and port forward the URL (I understand it's never just a "just"). I already have a camera, I can program WASD, and I could put a bunch of other things to fulfill a greater operation: bother the absolute heck out of my brother. I know you're reading this bro, this camera and controls are only going to be the BEST of your annoyances.
